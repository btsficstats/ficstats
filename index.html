BANGTAN AO3 DATA ANALYSIS 2021
made by twitter user btsficdata

Hello and welcome to the yearly bangtan ao3 overview!

Yes, I made a website this year. The reason is that I wanted to have interactive charts, instead of just some Google sheets screenshots. This way, you can see the data YOU want to see :) You can click the chart legend on the right side of every chart to show or hide data from the charts. ALT-click will hide everything but the dataset you selected. Have fun!

Please note: you can screenshot / cross post whatever you want, but I would love it if you credit me (@btsficdata) or link back to this website somewhere :)

Note: the pie chart, while sort of helpful as a visualisation, is inherently a bit flawed as the total of fics here is over 100%. This is because multiple ships can occur on the same fic.
