Overall

In 2022, in total, 35.106 fics were written in the BTS fandom. A lot has happened in the past year, and it's not that surprising that a lot less fic was written compared to previous years! Here’s the overall progress of the total amount of fics.

Of the 35.106 fics, 24.176 were finished, which is just shy of 69% (nice). Once again congrats to everyone who contributed to this, both writers and readers!

Ships

This year's ship stats are not all that surprising if you've been around the BTS ficdom for a while. Taekook still steadily takes the lead, with jikook and yoonmin close behind.

Note: the pie chart, while sort of helpful as a visualisation, is inherently a bit flawed as the total of fics here is over 100%. This is because multiple ships can occur on the same fic.

Let's look at relative growth! For the record, you read this chart as “we wrote x% more/less of this ship this year”. Note that this percentage will always look higher on small numbers.

2seok has known the biggest increase, jumping by almost 10% -- visible in the ship ranking as well, where they jumped up 3 places! The biggest loss goes to vmin, who for the first time ever didn't make the top 6 this year.

Reducing this data per member (the number of fics where this member is part of the pairing) gives us the next chart.

The distribution of ot3 fics looks like this: like any other year, maknae line leads the chart by a huge amount, followed by yoonminhope and rapline.

And for fun, member “harem” fics. Also as usual, the ficdom seems to love everyone loving on the maknae the most.
