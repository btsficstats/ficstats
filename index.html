Overall

In 2023, in total, 28.245 fics were written in the BTS fandom. This brings us back to pre-2018 levels. This is not entirely surprising, but seeing the progress chart is definitely interesting.

Of the 28.245 fics, 19.032 were finished, which is about 67.4%. A bit lower than last year, but not by much. Congrats to all writers for contributing!

Ships

What's there to say about the ship charts that hasn't been said yet (by me) (many times)? I am convinced Taekook will take the lead of every chart until the end of the world and time and everything.

Note: the pie chart, while sort of helpful as a visualisation, is inherently a bit flawed as the total of fics here is over 100%. This is because multiple ships can occur on the same fic.

Relative growth is a chart I personally have mixed feelings about every year. You read this next chart as “we wrote x% more/less of this ship this year”. This is a percentage that will always look higher on small numbers, so it looks really high on smaller ships while it really only had a small increase or decrease. In short, don't take this one too seriously..

Ship data per member (the number of fics where this member is part of the pairing) gives us the next chart.
